## Brand Rules
- Never crop the curls.
- Keep the engagement ring visible when naturally appropriate.
- Preserve authentic facial structure and freckles.
- Prioritize warmth, expertise, and approachable professionalism.
- Every image should answer: 'Would I trust her to help me build a wardrobe?'
