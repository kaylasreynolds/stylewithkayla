A Little More About Me

I love to read. I love to sew. I love to build, create, and bring ideas to life with my hands. There’s nothing more satisfying than a tangible project.

I love the quiet of the forest and the peace of a beach, but I’m also very much an indoor cat.

My life revolves around my husband, our nephews, Harry (the Maine Coon) and Fluffers (the tabby).

My friends would say I’m the pink girl, the tiny things girl, the one who notices the little details and finds joy in them.

I’m an extroverted introvert—witty, sarcastic, sentimental, and a total Type A.

I could eat Mexican food every day and I have a million random playlists for every mood.

Why I Do This

Style should support your life—not make it more complicated.

I’ve been there—the weight fluctuations, the frustration of nothing fitting, and the feeling of standing in your closet thinking, “I have nothing to wear.”

I get it. And I’ll never take for granted the trust it takes to let someone help you through that.
