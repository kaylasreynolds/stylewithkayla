Hi, I’m Kayla

Personal styling with a thoughtful, real-life approach.

I’m a Personal Stylist at Macy’s Boise Towne Square, and I help women and men feel more confident, prepared, and at ease when they shop.

My goal is not to change who you are or push you toward a specific look. It’s to understand your life, make the process easier, and help you find clothes that genuinely feel like you.

Why I Do This

Style should support your life—not make it more complicated.

Shopping can feel overwhelming when you’re short on time, unsure about fit, dressing for a new season of life, or simply tired of searching through endless options.

That’s where I come in. Before your appointment, I learn what you need and prepare options with intention. During your visit, we work together at your pace—trying pieces, adjusting the direction, and creating outfits that make sense for your lifestyle, comfort, and budget.

You’ll always receive honest guidance in a relaxed, judgment-free environment. Appointments are complimentary, and there is never pressure to purchase.
